GUIDELINES

Given below are the guidelines for when components / equipment is(are) borrowed from The Hardware Shop at Innovation Garage:-

  • For each component borrowed from The Hardware Shop, Rs. 100 (Rs. 50 + Rs. 50) (Fee + Caution Deposit) will have to be paid OR 5% of the MRP of the component (larger amount taken into consideration).
  • The duration alloted is 2 weeks.
  • The Caution Deposit will be returned once the component is given back.
  • If the component is damaged, the borrower is liable to pay full MRP of the component to Innovation Garage.
  • FINE: For every week after the alloted 2 weeks, Rs. 10 will be deducted from the Caution Deposit.
  • RENEWAL: The component must be renewed for use every 2 weeks.
  • The amount to be paid is the updated Fee.
  • After each renewal Rs. 10 is added to the Fee.

Given below are the guidelines for when components / equipment is(are) purchased from The Hardware Shop at Innovation Garage:-

  • The mentioned prices of the available components are non negotiable.
  • Once handed over, The Hardware Shop takes no responsibility of the functioning of the component (apart from a select few components).
